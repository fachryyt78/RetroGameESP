    SETTINGS

    HOW TO PLAY

    TruckaJumpa is the sequel to Frogga. Your truck is fixed at the start of the track. Barriers move from right to left. Press jump (Space or Up) to leap over them. If you're on the ground when a barrier reaches your position, you crash and lose a life. Clear barriers by jumping over them to score. Higher levels increase obstacle speed and spawn rate.

    TIPS

    Time your jump so you're in the air when the barrier passes. Jump duration is fixed — don't jump too early or too late. Watch the closest barrier and jump when it's one or two cells away (depends on speed).

    LEVELS

    Level 1: Slow obstacles. Level 2–4: Faster. Level 5+: Denser and quicker. Spawn interval shortens every few levels. No level cap.

    KEYBOARD

    SPACE or Arrow Up — jump. P — pause/resume. All other keys ignored during play.
